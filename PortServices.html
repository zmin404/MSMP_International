Containerized Cargo Handling Service

Mandalay Semeikhon Port working with several shipping lines and Yangon Ports to have regular containerized cargo shipment between Yangon and Mandalay. With the advance port facilities, Mandalay Semeikhon Port able to handle both 20’ and 40’ container. The container storage yard with RMG crane installed provided container storage of up to 1000 TEU. Mandalay Semeikhon Port encourage cargo owner to store the containers in Mandalay Semeikhon Port for delivery to Yangon.

With the support from the Inland Water Transport Department, containerized cargo throughput via Ayerwarddy River will increase in the coming years. This is also in line with the government’s direction to ease the congested traffic in Yangon city and Yangon to Mandalay highway, which to encourage ‘Green Transport’ by river between Yangon ports to Mandalay Semeikhon Port. The switching from land transport to river transport will minimize the road accident and to protect road user’s safety as well.

First Container Barge called to SMP

In March 2016, the first container barge berth at Mandalay Semeikhon Port, under JICA funded project. The container barge owner found it is very convenient to discharge container in Mandalay Semeikhon Port.

General Cargo Handling Service

With the fast economic development momentum in Mandalay region, the transportation volume of construction material, machinery, telecommunication infrastructure and other general goods are increasing from year to year. The delivery of river transport for general cargoes playing an important role in transporting large quantity of material from Yangon to Mandalay and return with finished goods for export or local use.

Mandalay Semeikhon Port provide effcient handling for General Cargo discharge and loading. The first completion of operation enable cost saving for General Cargo owners.


Bulk Cargo Handling Service

The best way for bulk cargo transportation from Yangon to Mandalay is by river transport. Mandalay Semeikhon Port provide handling facility for all type of bulk cargoes. The cargoes handle in Mandalay Semeikhon Port including coal, fertilizer, sand, stone, iron ore and beans. With the special designed Bulk Cargo Grapple (BCG) in the port, the discharging operation for Bulk Cargo operate efficiently and faster.

Cargo storage Service

Different cargo owner require different needs of cargo yard to store their cargoes. With the huge open yard space available, Mandalay Semeikhon Port is the only port in Mandalay region to provide cargo storage facility to cargo owners. The storage yard providing convenience to cargo owner to store their cargo in the port before loading onto the barge, or for cargo discharged from the barge.

Warehousing service

The proposed warehouse in Mandalay Semeikhon Port will provide convenience to cargo owners to store rain protective cargo such as cement and sugar bags. The cargo handling for the barge will be efficient and without delay with the use of warehouse storage facility.

Cargo Weighting Services

Mandalay Semeikhon Port also provided cargo weighting service to cargo owner. The electronic weighting scale able to weight the cargo from 2 ton to 50 ton. Port staff will record down the weight for the cargo before discharge or loading operation.

Container Repair and Cleaning Service

As the only one port in Mandalay to handle containerised cargo, Mandalay Semeikhon Port provide container repair and cleaning service to cargo owner and shipping agent. This given convenience to shipping agent to use Mandalay Semeikhon Port as a transhipment hub without worry.

Door to door cargo trucking services

To provide one stop service in logistic sector, Mandalay Semeikhon Port has engaged with Kyaw Logistic services to provide cargo delivery service from the port to customer delivery factory within Mandalay area. Cargo owner no need to worry to find for trucking company as cargo trucking service available in the port.

Barge Charter Services

Mandalay Semeikhon port also provide barge charter service for cargo owner. This providing cargo owner convenience in transporting cargo by river from Mandalay to Yangon
